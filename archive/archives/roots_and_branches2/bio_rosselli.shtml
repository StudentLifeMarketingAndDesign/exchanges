Diana Thow

Diana Thow recently completed her MFA in literary translation at the University of Iowa with a thesis on Amelia Rosselli. She has published her work in Carte Italiane, Mare Nostrum, 91st Meridian, Words Without Borders, House Organ and The Columbia Review. She is a postgraduate writing fellow at the University of Iowa, whereshe teaches a course on literary translation.
