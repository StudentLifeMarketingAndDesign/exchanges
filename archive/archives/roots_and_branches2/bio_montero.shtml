Michelle Gil-Montero

Michelle Gil-Montero is a poet and translator of Latin American poetry. Her poems and translations have appeared or are forthcoming in Colorado Review, Jacket, Conjunctions, Third Coast, and others. She is an Assistant Professor of English at Saint Vincent College.

 

María Negroni

María Negroni’s previous books in English translation are Islandia (Hushion House, 2001; trans., Anne Twitty) and Night Journey (Princeton University Press, 2002; trans., Anne Twitty), a book that won Negroni an Argentine National Book Award. Her other books in Spanish include: De tanto desolar, Per/canta, La jaula bajo el trapo, Diario Extranjero, Camera delle Meraviglie, and La Anunciación.
