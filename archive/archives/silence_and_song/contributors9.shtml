Katherine Perry

Katherine Perry will receive her MFA in fiction writing from the University of Iowa in May 2008. She has published previously in eXchanges, and has original fiction in Sub-Lit and notnostrums, though she would count learning to drive an automobile with a manual transmission as one of her most important Iowa accomplishments. She is currently attending to a newly developed obsession with the boll weevil infestation of the American South during the early 1900's.
