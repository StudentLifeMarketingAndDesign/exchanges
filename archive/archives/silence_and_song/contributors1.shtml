Guillermo Boido

Guillermo Boido is a professor of Mathematics and Science History at the Universidad Nacional de Buenos Aires and a member of CONICET, the National Research Institute of Argentina. He published several chapbooks in the early 1970s and, more recently, a collection of new and selected work, entitled La oscuridad del alba. Poemas 1970-2005. His verse has been translated into English, French, Portuguese and Italian.
