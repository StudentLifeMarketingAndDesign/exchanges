Liz Henry

Liz Henry has published poems, translations, and articles in Parthenon West, Xantippe, Lodestar Quarterly, Poetry Flash, Two Lines, Cipactli, caesura, other, Literary Mama, Convergence, and Strange Horizons. She blogs about poetics and translation at liz-henry.blogspot.com and is the Contributing Editor for Latin America at BlogHer.com.
