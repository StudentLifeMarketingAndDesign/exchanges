Katerina Stoykova-Klemer

Katerina Stoykova-Klemer was born and raised in Bulgaria. She immigrated to the United States in 1995, and since then, she has worked as a software engineer in high-tech companies. Presently she is an MFA student at Spalding University in Louisville, Kentucky. Her poems and stories have appeared or are scheduled to appear in a number of magazines, including Родна Реч (Native Speech), Пулс (Pulse), Черноморски фар (Black Sea Lighthouse), Литературен Вестник (Literary Gazette), Бургас Днес (Bourgas Today), Orpheus, Факел (Torch), Third Wednesday, and Anderbo.
