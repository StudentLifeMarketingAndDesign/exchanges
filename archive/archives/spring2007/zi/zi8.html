見 娘 喜 容 媚
願 得 結 金 蘭
空 織 無 經 緯
求 匹 理 自 難
初 寒 八 九 月
獨 纏 自 絡 絲
寒 衣 尚 未 了
郎 喚 依 底 為
寒 鳥 依 高 樹
枯 林 鳴 悲 風
為 歡 憔 悴 盡
那 得 好 顏 容
you see a cheerful lovely girl
and covet this gold orchid friend

your weaving lacks the warp and woof
to truly make the fibers blend

cold months of autumn
here I am alone
winding silk threads

winter clothes not nearly done
and there’s my man yelling
for the overcoat he needs
cold birds
huddle in the trees

melancholy winds
sigh through woods
bereft of leaves

lamenting our lost passion
this pale haggard look
will keep me well in fashion
