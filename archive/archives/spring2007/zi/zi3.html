light breeze at the month's beginning
beneath new woodland branches
bright flowers unfold
to the wind’s caress

(my lover
moved to play
spring's moonlight games
quietly removes my dress)
my green room's dark
as the month begins
silk curtains drawn
to winds gone crazy

cherishing spring
we can't stop singing
cinnamon wine
makes singing easy
