Ben Basan is a Ph.D. candidate in English at the University of Iowa. He was raised in Texas and England, but prefers to call Tokyo home. His research and day-to-day existence depend on intermedia, contemporary poetry and poetics, procedural architecture, nationalisms, anarchism, fresh coffee, and good food. He is Managing Editor of Factorial.org - an organisation and journal that promotes contemporary Japanese poetry in English translation.
