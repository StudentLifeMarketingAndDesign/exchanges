My prairie of nothing
my bowling green of snow
my field of vision
as far as the eye can see
my abyss from which rises
sand water
and earth in the long awaited
and quick spring
a flood of mountains also rises
toward summer
blocking out the sun more and more
it is swallowed elsewhere
bit by bit in its entirety
it is leaving for my own good
it will give me back my prairie
of snow and distance.
