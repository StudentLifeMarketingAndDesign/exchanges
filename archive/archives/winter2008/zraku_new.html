To Air

Used to the air’s trembling

from airplanes,

which will drop bombs

(elsewhere) –

despite revulsion, despite weeping,

used to –

so quickly used to –

also used to revulsion and weeping –

 

                              I want to tremble differently, like this air trembles,

                              and yet I tremble the same.

 

Air! Even with my cries

I can only make you

tremble –

even with my cries

I can only expel you –

only with my body

I still offer space

to you. How you tremble!

 

                              I want to tremble differently from how you tremble,


Black Square

Now we meet,

as things in trash

meet.

 

However, only what can break through

the horizon

of the possible, is possible.

 

The movement of a hand is inseparable

from the quick collapsing of whole layers

of the sky.

 

To survive until morning

is inseparable

from that which makes

 

a morning.   The sun from Victory over the Sun.

The situation,

when the movement of a hand (again – still – just now truly)

 

is an immovable line of the Black Square.

Zraku

Navajen na drhtenje zraka

od avionov,

ki bodo vrgli bombe

(drugam) –

kljub gnusu, kljub joku

navajen –

tako hitro navajen –

navajen tudi na gnus in jok –

 

                              hočem drhteti drugače, kot drhti ta zrak,

                              a drhtim enako.

 

Zrak! Še s svojimi kriki

samo povzročam,

da drhtiš –

še s svojimi kriki

te samo izganjam –

samo še s svojim telesom

dajem prostor

tebi. Kako drhtiš!

 

                              Hočem drhteti drguače, kot drhtiš ti,

                              a drhtim enako.


Črni kvadrat

Zdaj se srečujemo,

kot se srečujejo

predmeti v smeteh.

 

Samo, kar lahko prebije

horizont

mogočega, je mogoče.

 

Premik roke je neločljiv

od bliskovitega zrušenja celih skladov

neba.

 

Da se dočaka jutro,

je neločljivo

od tega, da se naredi

 

jutro.  Sonce od zmage nad Soncem.

Situacija,

v kateri je premik roke (znova – še – šele zdaj zares)

 

nepremakljiva črta Črnega kvadrata.
