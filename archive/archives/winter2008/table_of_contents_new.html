eXchanges is the University of Iowa’s literary e-journal devoted to translation both in and out of the English language. We aim to foster cultural interchange and expand awareness of translation as a valid art form. Publishing the source text alongside its translation, we also highlight the symbiotic nature of the process.

eXchanges would like to thank: the University of Iowa Student Government, The University of Iowa Graduate College, The University of Iowa Department of Cinema and Comparative Literature, The International Writing Program, Christopher Merrill, Steven Ungar, Russell Valentino, Nataša Ďurovičová, and Becka Mara McKay for their support.

We would also like to thank the Mercè Rodoreda Foundation for permission to publish “On the Train.”
