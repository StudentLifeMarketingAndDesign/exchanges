We aim to foster cultural interchange and expand awareness of translation as a valid art form. Publishing the source text alongside its translation, we also highlight the symbiotic nature of the process.

Submissions for our spring issue will be accepted until January 15. The theme, which contributors may interpret as they wish, is SWEET & SOUR. Short stories, novel excerpts, literary nonfiction, and poetry are all welcome.

 

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Submissions should include both the original and the translation, biographies of both author and translator, and digital photos if possible. Translators should also include a few paragraphs describing their experience working with the original text. Submissions will not be considered without written permission of copyright.

Submissions should be limited to three per translator and total no more than ten pages in length.

Electronic submissions are preferred. Please send both original and translation as .doc attachments to eXchanges_journal@yahoo.com.

Direct paper submissions to eXchanges, E210 Adler, Iowa City, IA  52242, U.S.A.
We do accept simultaneous submissions; however, please inform us if your work is under consideration elsewhere.

For more information please vist eXchanges at www.uiowa.edu/~xchanges/ or email eXchanges_journal@yahoo.com.
