Lisa Rose Bradford

Lisa Rose Bradford teaches Comparative Literature at the Universidad Nacional de Mar del Plata and Translation Studies at the Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, Argentina. Her poems and translations have appeared in Poetry Now, Thorny Locust, Faultline and Calque. She has also published two books in Spanish on literary translation in Argentina, Traducción como cultura and La cultura de los géneros, and is currently working on an anthology of U.S. women’s poetry in Spanish, entitled Los pájaros, por la nieve for RIL (Chile), parts of which have appeared in Hablar de poesía.
