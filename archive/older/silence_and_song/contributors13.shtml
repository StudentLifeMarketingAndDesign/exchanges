Anna Guercio

Anna Guercio is a translator, poet, and PhD student in Comp Lit at UC Irvine. She got to know Antonio Ungar while working on her MFA in Literary Translation at the University of Iowa. Anna translates primarily from Spanish, has her fingers crossed for full-length publication of Ears of the Wolf, and thinks real hard about gender and lyric authority. This spring, her work will also be featured in Faultline and the Hispanic Review.
