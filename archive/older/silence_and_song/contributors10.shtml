Katerine Factor

Katherine Factor was raised across the river in Galesburg, Illinois, educated at Interlochen Arts Academy and Grinnell College.  A recent graduate of the Iowa Writers' Workshop, she is interested in eco-poetics and new natural histories.  Recent work appears in Diagram 8.1, Iowa Writes, and the Colorado Review.
