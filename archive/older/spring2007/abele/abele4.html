laika svelme saēd tavus gaisa dārzus
Semiramid
kā rūpīgus plūksnās caurumos vilnas paklājus
čaukstošas kodes

nakts noplēš deviņas vilkačādas
sasit dzeltaini rūsas pirksti
lai paliec kā karsta ogle
bezlietus tumsā

vīrs
kur bal viņa kauli
suns
mēle bez elpas elpa bez valgmes
zirgs
bez kustības
putni bez balss

vien tava verdzene
Semiramid
nemitējas nest tuksnesī ūdeni

sweltering time devours your airy gardens
Semiramid
the way that diligently burrowing fleecy holes in woolen carpet
moths rustle

night tears away nine werewolf hides
strikes stinging fingers of rust against each other
so that you remain like a hot coal
in the darkness of drought

man
where do his bones whiten
dog
tongue without breath breath without moisture
horse
motionless
birds lacking voice

only your slave-girl
Semiramid
incessantly carries water to the desert
